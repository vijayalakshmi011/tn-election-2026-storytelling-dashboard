# Tamil Nadu Election 2026: A Structural Political Realignment
📊 Project Overview

This project is a Power BI storytelling dashboard developed to analyze the Tamil Nadu Assembly Elections of 2021 and 2026.
The objective of the project is to transform complex election data into meaningful insights and uncover the key political changes that occurred between the two elections.
Rather than focusing only on election results, the dashboard explores:
- Constituency-level winner transitions
- Regional political shifts
- Changes in electoral competitiveness
- Party-level performance and seat distribution

The analysis reveals that the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election was not simply a change in electoral outcomes but a **structural political realignment** characterized by significant constituency flips, shifting regional strongholds, and increased competition.
🎯 Headline Finding

## The 2026 Tamil Nadu Election Was a Structural Political Realignment
Key Findings
------------
- Nearly 70% of constituencies changed winning parties
- Regional strongholds shifted across Tamil Nadu
- Average victory margins fell from 11.70% to 7.67%
- Electoral competition intensified significantly

 🔍 Research Questions Addressed
 --------------------------------
 1. Winner Transition Analysis
    In how many constituencies did the winning party change between 2021 and 2026?
    The dashboard investigates electoral volatility and major winner transitions across constituencies.
 2. Regional Political Landscape
    How did seat distribution shift across Tamil Nadu's regions between 2021 and 2026?
    The dashboard analyzes how party strength evolved across different regions and identifies significant geographic shifts.
 3. Victory Margin Analysis
    How did electoral competitiveness change between 2021 and 2026?

The dashboard evaluates:
-----------------------
  - Average victory margins
  - Strong mandates (>50% vote share)
  - Fragmented victories (<35% vote share)
  - Closest electoral contests

📈 Business Problem
---------------------
Election datasets contain thousands of records and multiple political entities, making it difficult to identify meaningful patterns through spreadsheets alone.
Decision-makers require a clear and intuitive way to answer questions such as:
  - Where did political power shift?
  - Which regions changed the most?
  - Which constituencies became more competitive?
  - How stable were traditional strongholds?
This dashboard addresses these challenges through storytelling-driven data visualization.

💡 Key Insights
----------------
 Constituency-Level Political Change
- 163 constituencies changed their winning party between 2021 and 2026.
- Electoral volatility reached 69.7%.
## Insight
Tamil Nadu experienced one of its most significant constituency-level electoral transformations in recent years.
## Regional Realignment
- Electoral changes were uneven across regions.
- Traditional party strongholds showed varying levels of resilience and decline.
### Insight
Political change was strongly influenced by geographic patterns.
## Increased Electoral Competition
- Average victory margins dropped from 11.70% to 7.67%.
- 83 constituencies recorded strong mandates.
- 66 constituencies were won with less than 35% vote share.
### Insight
The 2026 election was significantly more competitive than 2021.
